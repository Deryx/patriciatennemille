About Patricia

Experience

  • As a current Family Court Support Magistrate, determines issues of fact and law relating to Family Law, including, spousal support, paternity, child support, and violations, modifications and enforcement of court orders
  • Nearly 15 years of experience litigating matters as an attorney in the Family Court Bureau of the County Attorney's Office, relating to Abuse and Neglect, Visitation, Custody, Persons In Need of Supervision (PINS), and child support matters
  • Prosecuted domestic violence cases at Legal Services
  • Former Matrimonial/Divorce Attorney at Abel and Brustein-Kampel, P.C.
  • Former Certified Family and Divorce Mediator
  • Former Director of the County Office of Equal Employment Opportunity and Affirmative Action
  • Former Adjunct Professor of Legal Studies at Rockland Community College

In Her Own Words...

I am qualified to become the next Rockland County Family Court Judge because of my experiences and my passion relating to the area of Family Law. I am fair, honest, enthusiastic, and committed. I have great empathy and understand that, as a judge, I impact lives and, thereby, impact the community.

As a Family Court Judge, I would be in a unique position to ensure that families are supported and assisted. A strong family creates a strong community and, in turn, a strong community creates a strong society. I would be honored if you would allow me the privilege of serving the residents of Rockland County as Family Court Judge.
